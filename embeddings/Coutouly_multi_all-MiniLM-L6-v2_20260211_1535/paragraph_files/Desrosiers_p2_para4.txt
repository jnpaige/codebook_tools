 We propose a new approach to the study of
 lithic industries in the Arctic that employs
 the analysis of lithic technology as it has
 been developed in France. Our purpose in
 this paper is to evaluate the utility of this
 analytical approach to Palaeoeskimo lithic
 assemblages by employing it in our analysis
 of the collection recovered from GhGk-63.
