Given the technological approach that has guided the study of
Garba IVD, only results from similar analyses are compared in this
discussion, with the aim of adding new data to better deﬁne the
techno-economic patterns in East Africa between 1.76 and 1.40 Ma.
This discussion includes the following sites: Kokiselei 4 and 5 in
West Turkana, Peninj, EF-HR, FC West Occupation Floor, TK Lower
and Upper Floors in Olduvai, Nyabusosi 18, Gadeb 2E (due to the
uncertain time interval of the Gadeb assemblages between 1.45 and
0.70 Ma, we considered only Gadeb 2E as the oldest site, recently
revisited by de la Torre, 2011a).
