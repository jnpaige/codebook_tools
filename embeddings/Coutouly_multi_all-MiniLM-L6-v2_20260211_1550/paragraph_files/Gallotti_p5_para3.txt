A total of 9028 lithic objects (6986 knapped and 2042 unworked) were reanalysed in this work. They come from the artefact
collection (9821 specimens) stored at the National Museum in
Addis Ababa and at the Melka Kunture laboratory, which is almost
complete and remains accessible. A total of 499 obsidian objects in
the collection were not analysable because they are completely
broken. A further 6654 pieces recorded during ﬁeldwork and
