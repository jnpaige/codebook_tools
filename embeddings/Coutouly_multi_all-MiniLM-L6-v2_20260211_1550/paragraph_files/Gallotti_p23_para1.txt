In summary, it may be concluded that, except in the case of West
Turkana, predetermined methods appear systematically around
1.6e1.4 Ma at different sites, associated or not with LCT production.
Many cores showing a radial or centripetal exploitation are known
from previous sites, such as DK in Olduvai (de la Torre and Mora,
2005) and Gona (Stout et al., 2010), but they belong to methods
where core volumes and convexity conﬁgurations are not fully
managed and a hierarchy between a débitage surface and a prepared striking platform is absent. Only the status of the centripetal/
radial method in the older unit (E) at Garba IV remains unclear
(Piperno et al., 2009).
