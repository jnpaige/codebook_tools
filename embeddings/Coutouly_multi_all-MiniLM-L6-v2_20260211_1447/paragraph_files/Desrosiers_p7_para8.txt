The methodology developed in this
 field is strongly linked with the concept
 of chaîne opératoire, initially proposed by
 Leroi-Gourhan (1964) and developed
 further for lithic studies by Tixier and
 others (1980). This approach takes
 into account the whole process of tool
 manufacture, including raw material
 procurement, transformation steps, use,
 and final discard of tools permitting the
 reconstruction of the lithic technology
 knowledge or the prehistoric toolmakers'
 worldviews toward their technological
