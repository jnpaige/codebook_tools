Raw Material Management
 Quartzite and milky quartz were used
 in the Diana Bay area to make the same
 variety of tools manufactured of chert
 at GhGk-63 (Desrosiers 1986; Plumet
 1994). Milky quartz is the predominant
 raw material the in Kangirsujuaq area
 (ACI 2002; Gendron and Pinard 2000;
 Pinard 1997-1998). In Nunavik, it
 seems that the choice between quartzite,
 quartz, and chert was mainly influenced
 by regional availability (Gendron and
 Pinard 2000; Plumet 1981). However,
 further west on the Hudson Strait, so called "tan chert" prevails (Desrosiers
 and Rahmani 2003; Odess 1996: 107 109), while on the east coast of Hudson
 Bay, exotic chert dominates (Gendron
 and Pinard 2000).
