The differential selection and management of raw materials and
the local provisioning on the secondary sources for both chaînes
opératoires are observed, with or without transport from outcrops
to obtain large boulders for LCT production. The selection of
particular morphologies and lithotypes is not a new observation;
although published evidence remains limited, this behaviour has
been documented at older sites (Plummer et al., 1999; Hovers et al.,
2002; Stout et al., 2005; Goldman-Neuman and Hovers, 2009;
Harmand, 2009a, b).
