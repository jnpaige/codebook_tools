It must also be emphasised that a certain degree of the predetermination concept is revealed at Garba IVD and Gadeb 2E by two
different methods: discoid and unifacial centripetal prepared
exploitation. It has been argued that the latter method bears some
of the technical criteria successively developed by the Levallois
technology (de la Torre et al., 2003), whose Acheulean origin in East
Africa has been recently conﬁrmed at the Kapthurin Formation
around 200e500 ka (thousands of years ago) (Tryon et al., 2005).
This observation suggests that the co-existence of discoid and
Levallois technologies in more recent contexts could have an
antecedent in these Lower Pleistocene assemblages.
