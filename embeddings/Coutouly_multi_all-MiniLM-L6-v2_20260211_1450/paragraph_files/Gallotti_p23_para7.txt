In Olduvai, quartz and lavas do not seem to have been used
differently to manufacture speciﬁc components of the EF-HR
assemblage, despite lavas artefacts are more frequent than those
of quartz. Quartz predominates at the FC West Occupation Floor
and at TK for both chaînes opératoires. If a large part of the EF-HR
quartzes had a stream provenance, tabular blocks at FC West and TK
must have been transported from the original outcrops (de la Torre
and Mora, 2005).
