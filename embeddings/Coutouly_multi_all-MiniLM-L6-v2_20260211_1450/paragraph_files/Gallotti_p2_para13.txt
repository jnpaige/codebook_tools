Melka Kunture is located in a semi-graben depression that belongs to the Upper Awash Basin on the Ethiopian Plateau along the
western border of the Main Ethiopian Rift (Fig. 1a). The Oldowan
(Karre I, Gombore I, Gombore Ig, Garba IVG-E), Acheulean (Garba
IVD, Garba XII, Simbiro III, Gombore II, Garba I, Garba IIIE-B), Middle
Stone Age (Garba IIIA) and Late Stone Age sites (Woﬁ II, Woﬁ III,
Kella I) are located on both banks of the Awash river. The sub-actual
site of Balchit is located 7 km north of the previously mentioned
sites (Fig. 1b).
