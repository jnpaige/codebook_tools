- Selection of speciﬁc forms to produce ﬂakes with speciﬁc features.
A large set of cores displaying an unifacial unidirectional
exploitation produced elongated ﬂakes. Elongated cobbles
were selected, and the longest available surface was used as
the débitage surface. In this case, the initial morphology of
the cobbles required the rectiﬁcation of the striking platform,
creating a suitable angle to begin débitage.
