Stone working at Garba IVD is represented by small débitage
and large ﬂake/LCT production (SOM, Figs. 1 and 2). No spatial and/
or temporal fragmentation of these chaînes opératoires is documented. All production phases are present, a pattern expected for
in situ knapping processes. Both chaînes opératoires document a
single procurement system for the secondary sources near the site,
i.e., alluvial deposits and surface dispersion areas in the case of
