There are over 100 microblades/bladelets in 
the assemblage, 80 of which have been recorded 
and 18 are represented here (Fig. 9). None of the 
80 analyzed microblades exhibits intentional 
retouch on the edges nor are they transformed 
into tools (endscrapers, burins, etc.). Out of the 
80 microblades, 77 are made on chert (96.1%) and 
three on obsidian (3.9%). Moreover, out of the 
77 chert microblades, 49 are black or dark grey
