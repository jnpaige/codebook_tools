The stratigraphic sequence lies below the tuff of reverse polarity
described by Cressier (1980) as ‘tuff A0’ and most likely corresponds to the ‘small tuff below tuff A’ (Schmitt et al., 1977), which
was dated by K/Ar on glass shards at 1.30 and 1.46 Ma (Fig. 2b;
Raynal and Kieffer, 2004). Recent analyses dated this tuff to
1.429 (cid:1) 0.029 Ma (Morgan et al., 2012). Between archaeological
unit D and E, the Grazia tuff is dated to 1.719 (cid:1) 0.199 (Morgan et al.,
2012). Its polarity has not been established. However, the units
below and above are considered to be reverse (Fig. 2b, c; Westphal
et al., 1979; Cressier, 1980).
