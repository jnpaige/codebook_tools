Implements of Other Raw Materials
 Small quantities of other raw materi als are also present. Nephrite was used
 to produce three adzes (Figure 7: 4)
 and two burin-like tools by grinding.
 Forty-three flakes or fragments of
 hyalin quartz are present. Only five
 small fragments of grooved soapstone
 were found, but cannot be linked to
 container manufacturing. Finally, two
 granite hammerstones were identified,
 along with 26 pieces of other unidenti fied materials.
