Garba IV is located on the right bank of the Awash at the outlet
of a small tributary of the Garba creek (Fig. 1b). The site was
discovered by Chavaillon in 1972 and excavated from 1972 until
1982 under the direction of Piperno (Piperno and BulgarelliPiperno, 1975; Piperno and Bulgarelli, 2004). Several archaeological horizons were discovered: C, D, E, F and G.
