Crystal Quartz Implements
 Quartz was present in the form of small
 hexagonal crystal and hyalin quartz con cretions (n = 105; 0,9%). This material
 was used to produce three kinds of tool:
 tanged microblades (n=9, Figure 5: 6),
 lateral retouched microblades (n=2),
 and end scrapers (n = ll). Two natural
 crystals were also found at the site, and
 these may have been kept for later tool
 manufacture.
