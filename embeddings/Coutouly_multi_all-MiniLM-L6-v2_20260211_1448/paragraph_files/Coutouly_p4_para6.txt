represent a small fraction of the assemblage. The 
existing NPS catalog for the Amakomanak site 
shows 741 catalog entries (with a total of 1,557 
artifacts), all of them lithics. Most of the artifacts 
are chert, and less than 6% of the artifacts made 
of quartz, quartzite, obsidian, limestone, or other 
stone. Most of the chert is white, gray, dark gray, 
or black in color, with most suspected to be from 
the Brooks Range region (although specific sources 
are unknown). Of particular note, the black chert is 
probably of nonlocal origin (Gal, personal communication 2006). Chert variability and sourcing 
is poorly known in the region, although this issue 
does not impact the present analysis.
