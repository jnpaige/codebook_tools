Some of the criteria used at Garba IV to turn large ﬂakes into
LCTs, i.e., a thick and wider-than-long ﬂake, are shared at all the
other sites with LCTs produced from large ﬂakes. Nevertheless, the
use of large ﬂakes is not systematic and large cobbles could also be
directly retouched or shaped, as demonstrated at Kokiselei 4, Gadeb
2E and Olduvai (de la Torre and Mora, 2005; de la Torre, 2011a;
Chevrier, 2012).
