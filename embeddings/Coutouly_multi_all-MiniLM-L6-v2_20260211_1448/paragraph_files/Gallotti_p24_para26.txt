It seems clear that the classical label of Developed Oldowan,
proposed by the previous studies to classify this industry and
exclusively based on typo-metrical features, does not account for or
deﬁne all of the technical elements issuing from this study. The
techno-economic traits of the Garba IVD industry conﬁrm the existence of a common technological background in East Africa between 1.76 and 1.40 Ma, despite presence/frequency of certain tool
types. Further techno-economic analyses of the penecontemporaneous sites of Melka Kunture will allow for the better evaluation of
the status of Garba IVD and the evolutionary tendencies in a homogeneous micro-regional and environmental setting.
