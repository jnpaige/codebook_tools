Small débitage is the major component of the lithic assemblage.
Flakes were the intended end product, and they were seldom
retouched. There is no correlation between a speciﬁc product shape
or size and the blanks that were turned into retouched pieces.
Retouched small tools are of irregular shapes, and morphological
standardisation is absent: retouch was not undertaken to obtain
speciﬁc forms but only to modify edges.
