The Awash destroyed an unknown portion of the northern part
of the site, and the Garba creek washed away the central part of the
unit D. The excavation area (approximately 100 m2) of this unit is
therefore divided into the Eastern Sector (ES) and the Western
Sector (WS), which were excavated in 1972e1975 and 1975e1982,
respectively (Fig. 3a; Piperno and Bulgarelli, 2004).
