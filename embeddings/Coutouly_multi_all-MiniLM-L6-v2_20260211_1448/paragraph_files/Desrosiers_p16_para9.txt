End scrapers
 Eleven of the 33 quartz flakes were trans formed into end scrapers. Seven of these
 were prepared by completely retouch ing the initial blank, the remainder
 by retouching only the distal portion.
 Very small end scrapers are absent. The
 blanks used for end scraper production
 are thick flakes and do not appear to
 have been specially produced for that
 purpose. As for chert end scrapers,
 it appears improbable that there was
 systematic flake production to obtain
 end scrapers in quartz. We therefore
 suggest that the blanks were by-products
 resulting from the production of micro blades.
