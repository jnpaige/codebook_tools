The Microblade Component 
from Amakomanak
There are 14 microblade cores, all made of chert, 
which can be classified into three groups: black 
chert (n=6, 42.8%), gray chert (n=5, 35.7%), and 
dark-gray chert (n=3, 21.4%). The size of these 
cores varies from 26 to 50 mm in height, from 
19 to 55 mm in length, and from 18 to 37 mm in 
width (Table 1).
