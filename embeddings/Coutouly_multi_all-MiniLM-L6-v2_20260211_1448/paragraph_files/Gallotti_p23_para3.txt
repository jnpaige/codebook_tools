At RHS-Mugulud and MHS-Bayasi in Peninj, the co-occurrence
of alternative chaînes opératoires is linked to a dichotomy in the
use of raw materials. Small cores and ﬂakes were made from smallmedium-size cobbles of quartz, basalt and nephelenite, whereas
LCT blanks were obtained from basalt boulders, abundantly scattered in the nearby Sambu volcano piedmont (de la Torre et al.,
